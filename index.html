What is Body Mass Index (BMI)?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a widely used health measurement that helps determine whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height. It is calculated using a simple formula:
BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height² (m²)
BMI is commonly used by health professionals to screen for weight categories that may lead to health problems such as heart disease, diabetes, and high blood pressure.

Understanding Your BMI Result

  • Underweight: BMI is less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI is 18.5 to 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI is 25 to 29.9
  • Obese: BMI is 30 or more
